Education

This section is about my education. Having the chance living in different countries, I experienced learning in different schools from countries that I had lived. Born in Taiwan, I had my preschool there. My mother heard that education in Singapore is much better, so she brought me to Singapore when I was second year in my preschool. However, I was brought back to Taiwan for my third year in pre-school. After I graduated from preschool, I was lucky enough to move to Japan and start my elementary school there. Within 8 months or so, I had to move to China with my family due to my father's job relocation. Thus, I had my grade 2 and 3 in China. At the end of grade 3, I moved back to Japan again and I finished my elementary school there. After my graduation, I went back to Taiwan, my home country, for vacation and then moved to Vancouver in Canada. Interestingly, Canada have 7 years of elementary school. Thus, I had my second elementary school graduation there. My high school life was also in Vancouver. It was pretty fun until I graduated in 2014. In my grade 12 year, I was successfully accepted into University of Waterloo and I moved to Waterloo, Ontario, in 2014 where I started learning programming.
